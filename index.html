CELEBRATING 75 YEARS OF GLORY AND EXCELLENCE

What a special occasion this is! As we commemorate the 75th anniversary of our JNTUA-ANANTAPUR, this is indeed a time for congratulations—a time to commend all the faculty and staff who have worked so diligently over the years to develop this college into an essential element of our University. And time to applaud the many alumni who proudly identify themselves as JNTUA graduates—they serve as proof positive of the College’s continuing success. It is also time to express a heartfelt thank you to the countless supporters and students who have provided assistance and inspiration along the way. For three-quarters of a century, Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (Anantapur) has steadfastly embraced its commitment to educating .Today, with many students eagerly pursuing degrees within its classrooms and labs, the College of Technology has never been more relevant or more recognized. So, let us celebrate its past with great respect and look forward to its future with great enthusiasm .
